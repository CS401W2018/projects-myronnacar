Portrait Photography.

Portrait photography is a type of photography that focuses on capturing a person's face and personality. The goal is to show who they are by highlighting their expressions, emotions, and features. It can be done in many settings, like a studio or outdoors, and can be formal or natural. The main idea is to create a photo that represents the person in a meaningful way.
