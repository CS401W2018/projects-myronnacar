Why 313Studio.?
313 isn't just a number—it's the house number of my home in Cebu, Philippines, where my love for photography began.
KAHUKU, HAWAII
Hey, I'm Myron!

I'm the photographer behind 313Studio.

I've always enjoyed finding beauty in the everyday, but it wasn't until the pandemic that I discovered my passion for photography. Like many others, I was looking for something meaningful to do, and that's when I found YouTube tutorials about photography. I got hooked right away.

I didn't have fancy cameras or equipment, but that did'nt matter. With just my mobile phone, I started taking photos and realized how much I love capturing moments, telling stories, and seeing the world in a new way.

I'm a beginner photographer, now based in Hawaii, where I study at Brigham Young University-Hawaii. I'm majoring in Information Technology, but photography remains a big part of my life. As a Visual Arts minor, I'm excited to take courses in photography and continue learning and growing in this art form.

313Studio. is proof that you don't need expensive gear to make art—just passion and creativity. Photography, for me, is about finding beauty in the ordinary and sharing those stories with others.

My Dream Camera.

While I currently use my mobile phone for photography, the Fujifilm X100VI is my next big goal.

The sleek, retro-inspired body that is both lightweight and portable, makes it ideal for urban photography and travel. Its design makes photography feel as much an art as the photos themselves. The film simulation modes allows for a nostalgic, film-like aesthetic straight out of the camera.

Owning the Fujifilm X100VI would be a milestone in my journey as a photographer. For now, it serves as a source of inspiration, motivating me to keep improving my skills and building my portfolio until the day I can make this camera a part of my creative process!
