Tips and Tricks for Mobile Photography
Technique Description How to Apply
Focus Lock Prevents the camera from refocusing when you tap. Tap and hold on your subject to lock focus, then shoot.
Leading Lines Uses lines in the photo to guide the viewer's eye. Find natural lines (roads, fences) and align your subject along them.
Natural Light Utilizes sunlight or available light for better clarity and tone. Shoot during golden hour or place your subject near a window.
Portrait Mode Creates a blurry background (bokeh) effect. Activate portrait mode on your phone to emphasize the subject.
Rule of Thirds Aligns key elements along grid lines for balanced shots. Enable the grid lines on your camera and place your subject off-center.
Close-Ups Captures details by getting closer to the subject Physically move closer to your subject for intimate shots.
Wide Angle Captures more in a single frame, great for landscapes. Use the wide-angle mode on your phone or step back for more coverage.
