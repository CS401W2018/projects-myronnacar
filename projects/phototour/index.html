I have always wanted to visit Canada because of the beauty of its nature, from its mountains to its serene lakes and lush forests. The country's breathtaking national parks offer endless opportunities for adventure and the chance to witness the Northern Lights in the more remote northern regions. This photo tour is my way of capturing and sharing the natural wonders that make Canada such an extraordinary destination for nature lovers.
