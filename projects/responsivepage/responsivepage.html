This is Bluey's Family

This is a photo of Bluey's Family in their front yard.

  • Bluey's family are all Blue Heelers.
  • Their family loves imaginative play.
  • Bluey's family reflects the experiences of real-life families.

Bluey Heeler

Bluey Heeler, the main character of the animated series "Buley", is a lively, six-year-old Blue Heeler. She enjoys trying new things, from exploring new places to creating intricate games. Her adventurous nature also helps her learn about the world around her.

Bingo Heeler

Bingo Heeler is Bluey's little sister. Bingo is an adorable and thoughtful four-year-old Red Heeler with a gentle and quirky personality. Bingo has a vivid imagination and loves playing games that involve nature and animals.

Bandit Heeler

Bandit Heeler is Bluey and Bingo's Dad. He is a playful father and share a very special bond with his daughters. He often joins Bluey and Bingo in their imaginative games. He also has a very good sense of humor.

Chili Heeler

Chili Heeler is Bluey and Bingo's Mom. She is warm, loving, and wise. While Bandit is usually the one joining the kids in with their imaginative play, Chili joins whenever she can. Chili often addresses her daughters emotional needs and helps them process feeligns like dissapointment or frustration.
