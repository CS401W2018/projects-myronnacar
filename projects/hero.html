Interest Hero

Peter McKinnon

Peter McKinnon is a photographer, filmmaker, and YouTube content creator based in Canada. His YouTube channel is all about his photography and filmmaking journey. He also creates video tutorials about photography and film making. He also collaborates with different brands to showcase his photography. A very recent collaboration he did was with Red Bull, where he photographed an eclipse with two planes flying beneath it.

Prints
He is selling his limited edition prints online. The Bucket Shot 2 is now sold out!
Lightroom Presets
He is also is selling his Lightroom Presets online so photographers could apply his photo edits on their own photos.
Merch
He also has merchandise for sale online. Such as camera bags, lens covers, and stickers.
