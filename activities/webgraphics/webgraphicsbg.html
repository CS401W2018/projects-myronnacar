Engagement Questions

1. What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?
-photos that are large, complex, and detailed are better encoded using JPEG.

2. What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?
-simple images and animations are better encoded using GIF.

3. What is unique about the PNG format?
-PNG are unique because they offer lossless compression which means that no image data is lost even when the field is compressed.

4. How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?
-As a general rule, we should resize our photos to our desired size prior to importing them to our webpages. However, if we have to resize our image through css, we can assign either an ID or a Class to our image so we could adjust the width and height as we desire.

5. Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?
-The reason being is we don't want to use image that are too large that could consume too much memory and could slow down transfer over the network.
