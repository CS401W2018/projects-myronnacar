Block Tags

Engagement Questions

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?
-Block tags starts on a new line and takes up the full width available while inline tags, as the name implies, display content within the same line as surrounding text.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?
-Margin is the space around an element's border, while padding is the space between an element's border and the element's content. The border sits between the padding and the margin.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?
-We have absolute units which includes px, pt, in, cm, and mm. We also have relative units such as em, rem, %, ex, ch, and fr.

4. When would you use a figure tag?
-We used figure tag to encapsulate or to put non block Tags inside a content box.
